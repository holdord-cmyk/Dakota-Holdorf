Game Modes

starStory Mode

Guide AiAi and the crew through 10 worlds of increasingly tricky obstacle courses. Each world ends with a bonus stage where you can earn extra lives. The perfect place to start.

emoji_natureGolden Banana Mode

Take on 10 stages and collect every golden banana in each one. Hidden paths, tight time limits, and tricky placements make this mode a serious test of skill and exploration.

timerTime Attack

Race through preset courses as fast as possible. Speed runs, optimal routing, and muscle memory are the keys to dominating the leaderboards.

celebrationParty Games

Mini-games like Monkey Race, Monkey Fight, and Monkey Target let you compete with friends. Great for casual play and friendly rivalry.

Quick Tips
  • check_circle Use small, gentle tilts — big inputs cause you to lose control fast.
  • check_circle Study the stage layout before moving — plan your route in advance.
  • check_circle Tilt opposite your momentum before sharp turns to brake properly.
  • check_circle Different monkeys suit different stages — experiment with all four!
  • check_circle Watch speedrunner videos to find shortcuts and smarter routes.
  • check_circle Explore side paths off the main route to find hidden golden bananas.
